3CX Academy

The Department of Engineering and Technology at Southeast Missouri State University is proud to partner with 3CX to offer the **3CX Academy**. This program equips students with the essential skills to deploy, manage, and secure modern Unified Communications (UC) solutions, a critical component of today's interconnected business world.

What is 3CX?

3CX is a global leader in business VoIP IP PBX and Unified Communications technology. It offers an open-standards, software-based PBX that works with popular IP phones and SIP trunks, whether on-premise or in the cloud. Students in the 3CX Academy gain hands-on experience with a powerful, flexible communication system used by over 600,000 companies worldwide.

Learn More About 3CX Technology

Why Study Unified Communications?

In today's digital landscape, businesses require more than just a traditional phone system. Unified Communications integrates voice, video conferencing, live chat, mobile apps, and more into a single, seamless platform. Professionals with these skills are in high demand to help organizations improve collaboration, boost productivity, and reduce telecommunication costs.

3CX Certification Pathways

Our curriculum is aligned with the official 3CX certification program, providing students with a clear pathway to earning industry-recognized credentials. These certifications validate your skills and make you a more competitive candidate in the job market.

Focus: Foundational knowledge of deploying and managing a 3CX system. Topics include installation, firewall configuration, SIP trunks, extensions, and basic troubleshooting. This is the starting point for any 3CX professional.

Focus: Deeper understanding of more complex concepts. This level covers advanced extension settings, ring groups, call queues, digital receptionists, and reporting. It prepares students to handle more sophisticated business requirements.

Focus: Mastery of advanced system capabilities. This is the highest level of certification, covering topics like bridges, system security, failover, and advanced troubleshooting. Achieving this certification demonstrates expert-level proficiency.

SEMO Courses Integrating 3CX and VoIP Concepts

The skills you learn in the 3CX Academy are directly applicable to, and integrated with, several courses within the Department of Engineering and Technology curriculum:

  • TN425/TN625 - Wireless Communications and Mobile Data Networks: Explores how VoIP traffic is handled over secure Wi-Fi and cellular networks, including QoS (Quality of Service) considerations.
  • TN435 - Network Security: Covers securing UC platforms from threats, including SIP-based attacks, toll fraud, and ensuring encrypted communication.
  • TN475 - Enterprise Networking: Focuses on configuring switches and routers with proper VLAN segmentation and QoS policies to support reliable voice and video traffic.
  • TN495 - Cybersecurity Infrastruture Capstone Project: Students may design and implement a comprehensive secure communications solution for a real-world business scenario using the 3CX platform.
